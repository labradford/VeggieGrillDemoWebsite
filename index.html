Our Story - Taste and Believe

Veggie Grill was founded with a mission -- a desire for delicious, convenient food that you can feel good about eating.

We bring an unparalleled focus on flavor. We offer savory sandwiches and burgers, indulgent sweet potato fries, absolutely delicious desserts, all of which are plant-based.

Our delicious food is extremely satisfying without the cholesterol, animal fat, trans fat, and high fructose corn syrup that exists in other convenient food choices.

We are redefining the way people think about American comfort food. It can be completely delicious and satisfying. It is indulgent. It is savory.
